I make messy ideas real enough to test.

I am a French builder in New Jersey. Most of my work sits in the middle: between business and code, between the plan and the actual thing, between "this should exist" and "here is the first version."

I have spent 20+ years building products, teams, and engineering systems at CB Insights, Storyblocks, and now on my own. The useful part was never just the code. It was translating the mess into something people could see, use, and improve.

That work is now Ancre. I help non-technical founders take an idea that has been stuck in their head for too long and turn it into something real enough for another human to react to.
